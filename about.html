WELCOME TO ONEPOINT

OnePoint team works on finding and providing solutions to the most crucial challenges facing HR Management now a day, among these challenges: technological changes, workforce availability and quality concerns, company’s restructurings…etc. As companies modify priorities and operations, human resources functions can move from a dedicated HR role within the company, to OnePoint.

OnePoint provides different HR services more efficient and at lower cost than when done in-house, for the following reasons:

  • Exceptional response time to client queries
  • Unique flexibility in solutions provided to clients
  • Unparalleled professionalism and quickness with on-boarding of staff
  • On time reporting with planned and pre-agreed deadline schedules
  • We never miss payments / deadlines
  • Client portfolio and experience offer us a good reputation
  • OnePoint has expertise in different HR functions that is unavailable to many companies.
  • OnePoint reduces HR payroll costs.
  • OnePoint maintains more current systems and processes, so that the company does not have to keep buying new items.

OnePoint HR Solutions is established and still expanding company that offers its Clients top quality HR Solutions and Services that are tailored to specific clients and projects in Egypt, as well as the Middle East.

At OnePoint we passionately believe that every company has different needs. We therefore treat each project on an individual basis, with the overall objective of adding value to your business.

We are committed to producing the highest level of service and performance at all times, with total integrity and confidentiality. We unite talent with opportunities and strive to build the utmost professional Human Resources Development.
